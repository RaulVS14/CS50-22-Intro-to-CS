About me

I want the bulletpoints
Friendly greetings!

I was named Raul by my parents. Early in life I was interested in taking things apart and figuring out how things worked.

Childhood was in the theme of helping other people and solving problems for others.
I got my fair share of scrapes, bruises and scars. Inquisitive mind comes with its dangers after all.

The school was such a fun place for my curious soul. I found few friends, alot to learn and technology.
One time I was hidden away in computer class till 8 o'clock in the evening. Playing computer games and trying to solve problems in quests

After that I was hooked by computers. I remember truly enjoying animating car in the Word.

After high school in the university I discovered programming.
I found it really cool, because it was like magic - write a code and solve calculation assignment what took few days in an instant.

I followed my interest through different curriculums - physics, computer technology, technotronics.
Never quite finding the right one.

After a while I landed in the Tartu vocational school, learning web specialist specialty. HTML, CSS, JS, PHP and SQL.
It came with some challenges, but I managed to graduate.

New found skills obtained I managed to get job as a front-end developer in building guide company. I got to meet with clients and create user-interfaces.

Next job was STACC. It was quite different and with some new technologies. I applied for PHP dev, and I got to work on Magento plugin.
After the plugin was discontinued I moved on to full-stack development with Django, which is Python framework for web applications

I continue learning and gaining new experience. In 2022, working on CS50 course, learning C and algorithms.
